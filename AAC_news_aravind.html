Technology should be an important ingredient. It may be and should be for Social developement.

Dr. Aravind Vishnubhatla

Professor, Department of ECE, GRIET

Febraury 13, 2019

The seminar was about “Transform how the future’s predicted” in coordination with Dr.Arvind Vishnubhatla, Professor, Department of ECE and hosted by AAC on 13th February 2019, from 02:00 P.M. to 04:00 P.M.

It was attended by students interested in future technology. The seminar was attended by students in the bachelor’s program.

The seminar was led by Dr.Ramamurthy, Dean Advanced Academic Centre, GRIET and the organizing team introduced the speaker to the gathering.

The main objective of seminar is to present some valuable information regarding the Technology development in the rapidly transforming world.

This seminar was organized to provide insight and understanding regarding the latest developments in the field of future studies. Generate interest to plunge into future technologies. Help sharpen strategy skills. Provide foresight with the aim of improving the capacity of people to use the future to enable peace, inclusion and sustainability.

During his lecture he emphasized the need for teachers in the Universities and Institutes to keep their curricula up to date which will help in development of necessary skills to utilize technology very effectively. He also stressed that teachers should identify trends, technologies used in today’s world that will help students to remain competitive in the workface. He spoke about the need to transform ourselves and focus on the advancements in technology for a better future. Has enlightened students on future predictions of technology and has added to the path of learning.

Finally, the seminar ended with the official closing by the Advanced Academic Centre body. The seminar was well attended by college lecturers and students in the bachelor’s programs; around 100 participants were present. The participants expressed the desire that such lectures be arranged periodically to enable them to keep abreast of the developments in Science and Technology.
