It's going to be interesting to see how society deals with Artificial Intelligence, but it will definitely be cool.

Mr. Satish Yellanki

Corporate Trainer at Sathish Yellanki Institute of Software Solutions

March 18, 2019

The seminar was about Understanding Artificial Intelligence in coordination with Mr.Sathish Yellanki ,Corporate Trainer at Sathish Yellanki Institute of Software Solutions, and hosted by AAC on ,18th March 2019, from 1:00 P.M. to 4:00 P.M. It was attended by students aspiring to reap their careers in the field of AI. The seminar was attended by students in the bachelor’s program.

Dr.S.Ramamurthy, Dean –Advanced Academic Centre, opened the seminar and welcomed Mr.Sathish Yellanki by appreciating his work on bigdata and Machine Learning.

Sathish Yellanki,a high ranked person in Computer science industry. He initiated his career with programming in Assembly and scaled through different phases of programming using different language and in-depth implementation of Data structures. He made his presence in the world of internet programming by understanding the dynamics of IT industry. He has designed various domains (like Healthcare, Education, Retail...) and been a part of object relational, object-oriented data bases in design, analysis and coding. He was exposed to data ware housing and business intelligence domains. He even contributed his work in critical areas like performance, tuning, optimization of oracle database. At present, he is actively involved in the world of big data with Hadoop frame work, Hive etc. Apart from these responsibilities he is working with a team of professionals on Machine Learning aspects in the areas of agricultural datasets and health care implementations.

The main objective of the seminar was to share the functionalities, typologies and wide spread applications of AI.

At the seminar the presentation made by Mr. Sathish Yellanki includes the following information.

Artificial intelligence is a branch of science which deals with helping machines find solution to complex problems in a more human like fashion. AI systems are now in routine use in economics, medicine, engineering and the military. He explained the brief ideas of AI in various fields. Intelligence involves mechanisms, and AI research has discovered how to make carry out some of them. AI in the field of robotics is the make of human computational processes. The current intelligent systems have the capability of managing large data and simplifying complicated calculations. He also stressed about the need of AI in future. He suggested some AI based projects to the students.

Finally, the seminar was very interactive and around 80 participants were present.

Students were enlightened with clear understanding, about Artificial Intelligence. Students were provided with several ideas to implement using AI and were motivated to get into AI based real time project for better life.
