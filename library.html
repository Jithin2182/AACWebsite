AAC Digital Library

AAC digital library is where the information is digitalized and stored in the form of electronic portal that provides access to all kind of database such as bibliographies, full-text resources, catalogues, search engines, internet resources, reference works, E-journals etc. Earlier its only paper based works only available in libraries. However, technologies have overcome the traditional techniques and ways of gathering information from libraries. In the Current scenario, we have modest facility which is called AAC digital library. Hence, AAC digital library helps students for their learning and saves time instead of spending time to search information from hard copies physically.
