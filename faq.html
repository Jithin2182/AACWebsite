What is AAC?

AAC is Advanced Academic Center, an inter-disciplinary research center of GRIET, Hyderabad. Unlike all other student organizations, it is a sublime platform for the students with ingenious thinking who want to engage themselves in experimentation and research, making them explore the World of Advancements. Find out more with our About AAC

Peer-to-peer learning is fast, free and fun. Teaching peers is one of the best ways to develop mastery "It is a kind of educational practice in which students interact with other students to attain educational goals."Peer learning is where a student’s research, gather information and then share it with his fellow learner. It facilitates his learning and also that of his peers. The main benefits of peer teaching include, but are not limited to, the following: Students receive more time for individualized learning, direct interaction between students promotes active learning, and peer teachers reinforce their own learning by instructing others, students feel more comfortable and open when interacting with their fellow learner, peers and students share a similar discourse, allowing for greater understanding. This mean of teaching is a financially efficient alternative to hiring more staff members. Teachers receive more time to focus on the next lesson. Certain research indicates that peer learning activities typically yield the following results for both tutor and tutee: team-building and better relationships; greater psychological well-being, social competence, communication skills and self-esteem, higher achievement and greater productivity in terms of enhanced learning outcomes. Peer learning goes with the adage 'In learning you will teach and in teaching you will learn'.

What is AAC Digital Library?

AAC has come up with a new initiative that is AAC Digital Library. This library contains and is updated regularly with vast information of tech-tools, new trends in researches and advancements, published papers etc. which can be accessed from anywhere with some secured identification for professors and AAC students. This initiative means a lot for students to dream bigger and accomplish their goals.



Can everyone access AAC Digital Library?

No, the students who are the part of AAC – AAC students, AAC Committee, Faculty/Lecturers/Professors, Mentors etc… are allowed to use this tool. As it has secured authentication and requires Login ID & Password.
