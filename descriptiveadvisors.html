Advisors

Dr Jandhyala N Murthy
Director, Griet


Dr Jandhyala N Murthy studied PUC(MPC) from Maharajah’s College of Vizianagaram (1969-70) and then BTech (Mechanical) from IIT Madras (1970-75), MS (Thermal Power) (1982-84) and PhD (Thermal Power) (1985-88) from Cranfield Institute of Technology, UK. He served in the Maintenance Branch of the Indian Air Force as an AE(M) officer for over 25 years since 1975, held various appointments looking after operations, overhaul, training at unit, station and command levels.
He was an Instructor at Air Force Technical College, Bangalore and had a tenure at Combustor division, Gas Turbine Research and Development Establishment, Bangalore. He led an IAF Technical Training Team to Botswana, Africa, providing training and maintenance support to Botswana Defence Force. He is an alumnus of Defence Services Staff College, Wellington.
He took voluntary retirement from the IAF as a Wing Commander in 2001 and immediately joined as a professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (Autonomous), Hyderabad, an accredited and permanently affiliated college under JNTUH. He assumed the responsibility as the Director of GRIET since 31 January 2018, after a successful tenure as the Principal of GRIET since March 2004. His areas of interest span the Thermal Engineering domain, Gas Turbine Combustion Chambers, simulation and education.








Dr J Praveen
Principal, GRIET


Dr. J Praveen has done his PhD from Osmania University in power electronics, Hyderabad. His research work carried out at BHEL Research and Development Centre with support of University Grants Commission (UGC) junior research fellowship (JRF). He has Honor Code Certificate in course Circuits and Electronics 6.002X by Massachusetts Institute of Technology, USA (MIT) with ‘A’ Grade through edx. He received best teacher award and best project award from ISTE, He is a Certified Labview Associate Developer form NI,USA.

Shri Kidambi Seshadri
Scientist at NRSC ISRO/DOS


Shri Kidambi Seshadri did his Master’s degree in Geology from Osmania University, Hyderabad. He had a rich professional experience about 30 years in different positions of scientist’s grade and was responsible for a number of operational and research projects related to Geological, Remote Sensing, geophysical and allied areas. He was Head, Mineral Exploration Geo-environmental Studies (MEGS) & Scientist ‘SG’, Geosciences Group, Remote Sensing Application Area (RSA), NRSC ISRO/DOS, Hyderabad.
He is specialised in analysing different spectral regions data of remote sensing related to visual, short-wave infra-red thermal and microwave for geological applications. He was the project manager for many states of India in preparation of state-of-art groundwater prospects maps on 1:50,000 scale using Remote Sensing (RS) and Geographical Information System (GIS) techniques under a major national project -Rajiv Gandhi National Drinking Water Mission (RGNDWM). He was the recipient ‘ISRO award’ for 2007 under RGNDWM project based on his contribution under this project. He is Life Member in i) Indian Institute Remote Sensing (ISRS), Serving Humanity through Space Technology. Ii) Indian National Cartographic Association iii) Institute of Electrical Electronics Engineers Geology and Remote Sensing Society (IEEE, GRSS), Hyderabad Chapter.
